KasCo

KasCo is the new collaborative way of billing! KasCo does not believe in unfriendly payment reminders but in a cooperative way of billing where KasCo unites members who save money and time as KasCo takes care of the entire billing process from A to Z, including legal requests and the actual transaction of the money.
